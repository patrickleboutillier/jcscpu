ENABLER BUS RESETS
==================
Clear bus before ENABLER fires.
    $we->prehook(sub { $this->clear_bus_before_set(@_) }) ; 


STEPPER
=======
Send power signals "faster" to the 's' pins of the Ms so that the calculations happen in the right order.
    $wclk->prehook(sub {
        my $v = shift ;
        $wmsn->power(! $v, 1) ;
        $wmsnn->power($v, 1) ;
    }) ;
